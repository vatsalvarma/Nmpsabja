What’s Special About This Drink?

Nimbu Sabja is a refreshing blend rooted in traditional summer drinks. The tanginess of lemon awakens the senses, while sabja seeds — known for their cooling nature — gently soothe and hydrate the body.

When soaked, sabja seeds swell and develop a soft texture that makes the drink both interesting and satisfying. Shake & Gulp Nimbu Sabja brings this classic combination into a clean, ready-to-drink format. Since real seeds naturally settle at the bottom, every bottle is meant to be shaken well before drinking.

Who Should Drink This?

Nimbu Sabja is a refreshing drink for everyone as part of daily hydration.

It’s especially helpful for people who experience:

  • Excess body heat
  • Dehydration during hot days
  • Tiredness from heat or sun exposure
  • Mild digestive discomfort
  • A need for a light, cooling refreshment

A natural way to cool down and feel refreshed.
